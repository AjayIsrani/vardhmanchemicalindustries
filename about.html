WELCOME TO VARDHMAN CHEMICAL

We understand the role of
Premium Chemicals in Modern Construction

Vardhman Chemical Group is based at Ahmedabad (Gujarat-India), a place where the world comes together, being the second largest tiles manufacturing hub in the world, exporting to more than 150 countries.

Vardhman Chemical's Philosophy is to give value for money, believing that Quality is our Responsibility. On this core value, we provide effective construction solutions and continue to research for the right products for customers, instead of searching for customers for our products. This value-based business practices, made us one of the most sought-after company in Tiles & Construction Chemicals Industry in India and international markets like Europe, South East Asia, Latin America, Africa & Middle East.

Today, India is one of the fastest growing economies in the world and the infrastructure industry contributes a huge percentage towards the growth of our economy. Therefore, we focus to meet this ever growing demands of the industry by providing following products & services:

Tiles Concrete Repairs & Restorations Adhesives Screeding Water Proofing Sealants Grouts Flooring/Wall cladding solutions Admixtures Sealers & Cleaners Epoxy Textures

We have a highly trained team of professional applicators to meet all your needs of Smart water-proofing, Flooring Systems, Concrete Repairs etc , anywhere in India. This Technical Support System sets Marazen different from its competitors. Our qualified-team is consistently working together with Architects, Structural Engineers, Contractors and owners to ensure peace of mind from all construction hassles of our valued customer base.

Green Innovation

COMMITTED TO SUSTAINABILITY AND EXCELLENCE IN MANUFACTURING
Vardhman Chemical is commited to responsible and sustainable growth by adopting best practices and continually developing innovative and environmental friendly products. Our group manufacturing processes are certified to the ISO 9001:2005 International Quality Management Systems, and OHSAS 18001 Safety Management Standards guidelines.

With over 10 years of experience in construction chemicals, we collaborate with contractors and design professionals to deliver high-performance, durable solutions for diverse projects.
